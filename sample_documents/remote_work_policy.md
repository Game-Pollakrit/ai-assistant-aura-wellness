# Remote Work Policy 2024

## Overview

This policy outlines the guidelines and requirements for remote work arrangements at our company.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible to work remotely.

## Remote Work Schedule

Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval. The specific days must be agreed upon with your direct manager and team to ensure adequate coverage and collaboration.

## International Remote Work

International remote work requires **HR approval** and must comply with local tax laws and regulations. Employees planning to work from another country must:

1. Submit a request to HR at least **30 days in advance**
2. Provide details about the location and duration
3. Confirm compliance with local work authorization requirements
4. Understand that the company may not be able to accommodate all requests due to legal or tax implications

Maximum duration for international remote work is **4 weeks per year**.

## Equipment and Technology

### Company-Provided Equipment

The company provides the following equipment for remote work:
- Laptop computer
- External monitor (upon request)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls

### Employee Responsibilities

Employees working remotely are responsible for:
- Maintaining a secure and professional home office environment
- Ensuring reliable internet connectivity (minimum 25 Mbps download speed)
- Protecting company equipment from damage or theft
- Returning all equipment upon termination of employment

## Security Requirements

All remote workers must:
- Use the company-provided VPN for all work activities
- Enable full-disk encryption on all devices
- Use strong passwords and two-factor authentication
- Never share login credentials with others
- Report any security incidents immediately to IT

## Communication and Availability

Remote employees must:
- Be available during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM local time)
- Respond to messages and emails within 2 hours during business hours
- Attend all required meetings via video conference
- Keep their calendar updated with availability status

## Performance Expectations

Remote work is a privilege, not a right. Managers will evaluate remote work arrangements based on:
- Job performance and productivity
- Communication and collaboration with team members
- Meeting deadlines and deliverables
- Attendance at required meetings

Managers reserve the right to revoke remote work privileges if performance standards are not met.

## Policy Review

This policy is subject to change. Employees will be notified of any updates via email and company announcements.
