# Vacation and Time Off Policy

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Accrual Rates

Our company provides generous paid time off that accrues based on tenure:

- **0-2 years of service**: 15 days per year
- **3-5 years of service**: 20 days per year
- **6+ years of service**: 25 days per year

PTO accrues on a monthly basis and can be used for vacation, personal time, or sick leave.

### Carryover

Employees may carry over up to **5 unused PTO days** to the following calendar year. Any days beyond this limit will be forfeited unless approved by HR in exceptional circumstances.

### PTO Request Process

To request PTO:

1. Submit a request in the HR system at least **2 weeks in advance** for vacations longer than 3 days
2. Obtain manager approval before booking travel
3. Ensure adequate coverage for your responsibilities
4. Add your time off to the team calendar

Last-minute requests (less than 48 hours) require director-level approval.

## Holidays

The company observes the following **10 paid holidays** per year:

- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day

If a holiday falls on a weekend, the company will observe it on the nearest weekday.

## Sick Leave

Employees should use PTO for sick leave. If you are unable to work due to illness:

1. Notify your manager as soon as possible (before 9 AM)
2. Update your calendar and set an out-of-office message
3. Log sick time in the HR system

For extended illness (more than 3 consecutive days), a doctor's note may be required.

## Parental Leave

### Maternity Leave

Birth mothers are eligible for **12 weeks of paid parental leave**. This can be taken any time within the first year after birth.

### Paternity Leave

Non-birth parents are eligible for **6 weeks of paid parental leave**. This can be taken any time within the first year after birth or adoption.

### Adoption Leave

Parents adopting a child are eligible for **8 weeks of paid parental leave**.

All parental leave requests must be submitted to HR at least **30 days in advance** when possible.

## Bereavement Leave

Employees are provided with **5 days of paid bereavement leave** for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling). For extended family members, **3 days** of bereavement leave is provided.

## Jury Duty

Employees called for jury duty will receive full pay for up to **10 days**. Employees must provide a copy of the jury summons to HR.

## Unpaid Leave

In exceptional circumstances, employees may request unpaid leave. All unpaid leave requests require HR and director approval and must be submitted at least **30 days in advance**.

## Policy Compliance

Abuse of time off policies, including falsifying sick leave or taking unauthorized time off, may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
